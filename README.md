## Prototype Motor – Notes & Photos <a id="prototype-motor"></a>

When building this motor prototype, I ran into a few issues:

- **Conveyor belt material**: Started with plastic (cut & melted into a belt). It lacked friction/traction and had insufficient surface area contact.
- **Shaft**: Cardboard — too weak, fell apart easily, inconsistent performance.

Switching to a **shoelace** fixed most of the traction issue, but the mechanism design still needs work and sturdier materials.

[Video: Motor with Plastic Tubing (YouTube Short)](https://youtube.com/shorts/s_SNC1jV6XA)  
[Video: Motor with Shoelace (YouTube Short)](https://youtube.com/shorts/8Dqn5lWW97M)

**Materials used**: Cardboard, tape, double-sided tape, masking tape, motor, Arduino, breadboard, jumper wires.
