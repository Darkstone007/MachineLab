## Prototype Motor – Development Notes & Photos
When building this motor prototype, I encountered a few issues that need addressing:

- **Conveyor belt material**: Started with plastic material, cut and melted together to form a belt. It had insufficient friction for traction and not enough surface area relative to the available space.
- **Makeshift shaft**: Made from cardboard. Due to weak structure and poor material strength, it fell apart easily and performed inconsistently.

I could have benefited from a better mechanism design and sturdier materials. Switching to a **shoelace** solved much of the traction problem, but the overall design still needs revision.

Cyberpunk 2077 is a 2020 action role-playing game set in the fictional metropolis of Night City, California, within the dystopian Cyberpunk universe. It is known for its striking cyberpunk aesthetic.

**Key Prototype Features (Rough Sketch)**  
- **Night City background**: Buildings of varying heights with neon LED lights that flicker in sync with music and scenes. Some include billboards.
- **Train and car tracks**: Elevated tracks running through the air and behind buildings, using a conveyor belt mechanism for movement.
- **Main character**: Large figure hanging by a thread, moving up and down. Features cyberpsychosis — detached arm hanging limp.
- **Crowd**: Large group of people moving along a pedestrian path below, also using conveyor belt for dynamic motion.

![Rough Prototype Sketch](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9b03b08c-ef51-4508-979c-1f8521a458ba)

**Class Prototype Mechanism**  
This simpler mechanism represents the arm detaching and revealing another arm underneath. A string simulates pulling the detached arm upward. It connects the metallic arm on one end to the L298 motor on the other. When the motor turns, the arm is pulled back up to rejoin.
